Overview

INSPIRE, a technical community for engineering students from every domain.

We are tring to speaf this in Galgotias University which was established in 2011 with the spledid objective to work on innovative projects which will bestow creative minds so that students are ready to face all the hurdles in their way of learning new tactics.

MSP INSPIRE is a platform to promote and coordinate research and development by students, through student-industry interaction giving students freedom and opportunities to work upon diverse areas of technology and pursue their own ideas enabling them to become innovators. To enhance the technical advancement of our institute , we organize Seminars, Workshops, Hackathons, Techfest and many other competitions on various technical level in order to grasp the interest of the students towards technical aspects of their education.
